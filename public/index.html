Selling

Home staging, the art of making your home look neat, stylish and inviting to a potential buyer, is the essential first step to selling your home. Rudis Realty offers free staging advice that will help your home stand out from the crowd so that we may quickly match your property with the right buyer.

I will make a comparative market analysis of other area homes to ensure you receive the maximum profit.

Through the MLS listing and it’s many affiliate sites, I will offer your home to hundreds of prospective buyers, assuring you of the widest possible exposure in the market place.

I will keep you informed so that you know exactly where you are in the real estate process from start to finish.

I will direct you to professional advisors on the tax implications of selling. CPAs and 1031 Exchange companies can give specific advice to make sure you are making important decisions with clarity.

My Background

A veteran real estate broker, Michaela has earned the respect of hundreds of satisfied clients. She consistently delivers to buyers and sellers with committed efforts, expertise, and ethical service.

Growing up in California, and now living in South Lake Tahoe, Michaela has obtained a deep knowledge of the real estate opportunities here. And having also lived and practiced in Boston, Massachusetts for over 20 years, she has an insider’s knowledge of this dynamic and sophisticated city, too. Her vast experience and dedication will enable you to quickly navigate the challenges of buying and selling properties. She has cultivated a base of loyal and repeat clients by being prepared to offer candid guidance through all the issues that define each unique transaction. Unlike large franchises who will pass you from person to person, Michaela will remain with you every step of the way. You will be provided personalized attention to detail so that you feel confident throughout the buying, selling or renting process. Whether dealing in residential, investment, or commercial real estate, Michaela can help.

Michaela has been an active member of the professional real estate community. She has served on a number of committees for the Massachusetts Association of Realtors, including the Education Committee where she advised on the development of curriculum for commercial real estate courses statewide. Additionally, she has taught residential seminars for first time home buyers in the cities of Cambridge and Boston.

In Massachusetts Michaela has held a real estate broker license for over 20 years. In California she has been licensed since 2015 and has owned her home here in South Lake Tahoe since 2013. Michaela grew up visiting South Lake Tahoe regularly for ski vacations and is now thrilled to reside here.

In both Boston and South Lake Tahoe, Michaela has a particular passion for city safety and support for pedestrians, bicyclists, and all non motorized traffic. She is also an avid supporter of public transportation in the hopes of reducing motor traffic pollution and to preserve clean air and water. She is a longtime active member of the volunteer organizations “Livable Streets” in Boston and “Community Mobility” in South Lake Tahoe.
